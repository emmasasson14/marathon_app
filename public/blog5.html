Emma's 2023 NYC Marathon Journey Blog

Week 5

Posted February 1, 2023

Wow, a fifth week already completed. As the weeks go by, I am definitely starting to feel my body get stronger, and more used to running on a consistent basis.

This week was fun and yet challenging at the same time both physically and mentally. My feet have slightly become a bit more sore over the last few weeks and I am really working on maintaining my recovery routine where I make sure to include strengthening exercises, icing and stretching. I also tried to get new shoes this week, but they just did not fit right!! This is SO IMPORTANT. Make sure you get shoes that are best for YOUR feet.

Another challenge, but a bit more exciting was running in below 10℃ F at a whopping 6℃ F. WOW. I definitely was surprised at how warm I did feel... (though the 4 layers, gloves, head band and face mask did help a ton.)

That day was quite the chilly day, plus it was snowing which actually made it quite beautiful as well. I also got to run with a new running group right here in Denver, which was a nice change of pace... pun most definitely intended. To top it all off, I found a nice fireplace in a nearby REI right after my long run to thaw out!

Total mileage (walk/run) for the week: 7.23 miles
